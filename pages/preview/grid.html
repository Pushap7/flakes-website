Easy Grid

Simple grid is a collection of classes that you can use to build extremely flexible grid layouts. These grids are responsive and simple to implement.

Usage

Below is a simple 6 column grid and the markup used to create it. Elements inside the grid can span from 1 to 6 columns. Combined, they should not span more than the maximum number of columns. Our grid of 6 columns has 4 child elements spanning a total of 6 columns.

.span-1
.span-3
.span-1
.span-1
<div class="grid-6 gutter-20">
	<div class="span-1">.span-1</div>
	<div class="span-3">.span-3</div>
	<div class="span-1">.span-1</div>
	<div class="span-1">.span-1</div>
</div>

Use .gutter-n classes to space out your spans. All grids below have a .gutter-20 class to give a 20px gutter. Gutters can be 10, 20, 40, 60 or 80 pixels wide.
